Study Nest — University Study Partner Platform

Study Nest is a collaborative platform that helps university students find study partners, create study groups, schedule sessions, and track their learning progress.

Tech Stack: Angular, NestJS, Supabase

  • Match students with compatible study partners based on shared modules
  • Create and manage study groups and collaborative sessions
  • Track study progress with topic completion and hour tracking
  • Schedule study sessions with reminders and notifications
